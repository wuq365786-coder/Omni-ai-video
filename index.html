What Is Omni AI Video?

A practical AI video creation workflow for turning prompts, images, and short reference assets into video clips.

Omni AI Video is an AI video generation tool that helps you turn text prompts, images, and short reference assets into video clips. Instead of moving between separate tools for image animation, video generation, voice, sound, and editing, you can start from one place and create a short video draft faster.

Use Omni AI Video to test social video ideas, animate a product image, create an ad concept, draft a demo clip, or explore a visual story before committing to a longer production workflow.

Omni AI Video is best understood as a practical creation workflow. It should not be presented as a Google-owned product page or as confirmed access to any unverified third-party model capability.

Prompting

Write Prompts Like Short Scene Directions

Clear prompts help Omni AI Video understand what to generate. Treat each prompt like a compact scene brief.

Include

  • Subject
  • Action
  • Setting
  • Camera direction
  • Mood
  • Visual style
  • Audio direction
  • Dialogue in quotation marks
  1. "A skincare bottle on a marble sink, water droplets forming on the glass, slow macro camera movement, soft bathroom light, gentle ambient sound."
  2. "A food delivery app interface appears on a phone, the camera pulls back to show a busy lunch table, upbeat background music, clean commercial style."
  3. "A fashion sneaker rotates on a concrete floor, neon reflections, quick 10-second product reveal, energetic streetwear mood."
  4. "A teacher explains a simple science concept with animated visual elements in the background, friendly tone, clean educational style."
  5. "A travel creator stands on a quiet city street at sunrise and says, 'Here is the easiest way to plan a weekend trip,' with soft street ambience."

FAQ

Common Questions

Answers focused on inputs, outputs, formats, audio, usage, and product positioning.

What is Omni AI Video?

Omni AI Video is an AI video generator for creating short video clips from text prompts, images, and reference assets. It is designed for social videos, product visuals, ad concepts, demos, explainers, and creative tests.

Is Omni AI Video affiliated with Google?

Omni AI Video should be presented as an independent product. Do not describe it as Google-owned unless there is explicit confirmation. References to Gemini, Google, or related AI video technologies should be handled as separate context, not as product ownership.

Can I create videos from text?

Yes. You can start with a text prompt and describe the subject, action, scene, style, camera movement, duration, aspect ratio, and audio direction.

Can I animate an image into a video?

Yes. Image-to-video workflows let you upload a static image and generate motion from it. This is useful for product photos, character images, ecommerce assets, and visual references.

Can I use reference videos?

Yes. Reference-to-video workflows can use short video references to guide the generated result. Current product copy lists MP4 or MOV reference videos at 480-720P, 2-15 seconds, up to 50MB.

Does Omni AI Video generate audio?

Omni AI Video includes audio generation options and audio-related inputs. This makes it useful for short clips that need voice, ambience, music direction, or sound context. Results can vary by prompt, input, model, and settings.

What formats can I upload?

The current product copy lists JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WebP for images; MP4, MOV, and WebM for video; and WAV or MP3 for reference audio. Upload limits vary by input type.

What formats can I export?

Generated videos are exported as MP4 files, which can be used on social platforms, websites, ads, presentations, or in editing software.

How long can each generated clip be?

Current duration options include 5 seconds, 10 seconds, and 15 seconds. These options are best suited to short-form content, product reveals, quick social posts, and creative testing.

Which aspect ratios are supported?

Available aspect ratio options include Auto, 16:9, and 9:16. Use 16:9 for YouTube, websites, and presentations. Use 9:16 for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

What prompt style works best?

Short, concrete prompts usually work best. Describe the subject, action, setting, camera movement, mood, visual style, and any audio direction. If the clip needs dialogue, put the spoken line in quotation marks.

Can I use generated videos for commercial projects?

Generated clips may be used according to the product's current usage terms. Review those terms before using outputs in commercial projects.
